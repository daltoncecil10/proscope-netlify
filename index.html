ProScope Platform

Capture, Scope, and Share in One Field Workflow.

Built for adjusters and contractors who need faster documentation. ProScope combines pre-labeled photos, voice-to-note, built-in CRM, LiDAR tools, and scope-ready reporting.

Core Features

Everything here is based on live ProScope functionality.

Pre-Labeling + Voice-to-Note

Capture field notes quickly and keep photos organized by section and damage status.

Built-in CRM

Manage assignments, contacts, and claim details from one place in the app.

LiDAR-Enabled Scanning

Use LiDAR on compatible devices for enhanced field capture and measurement workflows.

Scope & Report Export

Build scope-ready PDF reports and review before sharing.

Shareable Report Links

Open report links in-browser for quick review by adjusters, office teams, and stakeholders.

Roof + Slope Workflows

Structured exterior, roof component, and slope capture flows keep documentation consistent.

How ProScope Moves Work Forward

1. Capture On Site

Start assignments, collect photos, add voice notes, and tag details while you inspect.

2. Build the Scope

Document roof, exterior, and component details with consistent structured inputs.

3. Share the Report

Export the scope PDF and share links for fast review and next actions.

Built Different
